## 1. Data Collection (jointhirdspace.app/privacy/data-collection)

# What Data We Collect

At thirdspace, we believe in transparency. Here's exactly what data we collect and why.

## Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Email address (for authentication and communication)
- Display name (optional, for personalization)
- Profile preferences (favorite venue types, vibe preferences)

**Activity Data**
- Vibe match selections (to improve recommendations)
- Check-in history (to track your exploration journey)
- Achievement progress (to award your milestones)

## Information We Collect Automatically

**Location Data**
- We collect your location ONLY when you actively use the app to find venues
- Location is used solely to find nearby venues that match your vibe
- We do NOT track your location in the background
- We do NOT store historical location data beyond the check-in venue

**Device Information**
- Device type and operating system (for app compatibility)
- App version (for support and updates)

**Usage Analytics**
- Feature usage patterns (to improve the app experience)
- Crash reports (to fix bugs and improve stability)

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Contacts or address book
- Photos or camera roll
- Microphone or audio data
- Health or fitness data
- Financial or payment information (handled securely by Apple/Google)
- Social media accounts or connections
- Browsing history outside the app

## How Long We Keep Your Data

- Account data: Until you delete your account
- Check-in history: Until you delete your account
- Location data: Only processed in real-time, not stored
- Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days

## Your Control

You can:
- Export all your data at any time
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Adjust location permissions in your device settings
- Contact us with any privacy questions

**Last Updated: January 2025**

thirdspace is a venue discovery app that helps you find places matching your current mood and vibe. We are based in the United States and can be contacted at hi@jointhirdspace.app.

thirdspace is the data controller responsible for your personal data collected through the app.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Data
- Email address
- Display name (optional)
- Venue preferences and settings

### Usage Data
- Check-in history
- Vibe match selections
- Achievement progress
- App interaction data

### Location Data
- Real-time location (only when actively using venue discovery)
- Check-in location verification

## Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:
- **Consent**: You consent to data processing when creating an account
- **Contract**: Processing necessary to provide our services
- **Legitimate Interest**: Improving our services and user experience

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide personalized venue recommendations
- To verify check-ins and award points
- To track achievements and progress
- To communicate important updates
- To improve our services
- To respond to support requests

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data only with:
- **Service Providers**: Cloud hosting (for app functionality)
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect rights

Our app integrates with:
- Google Places API (for venue data)

We retain your data until you delete your account. Upon deletion:

You have the right to:

- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data

## Children's Privacy

thirdspace is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## International Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.

## Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights:
- Email: hi@jointhirdspace.app
- Website: jointhirdspace.app

## 4. Security (jointhirdspace.app/privacy/security)

# How We Protect Your Data

Your security is our priority. Here's how we keep your data safe.

## Infrastructure Security

### Cloud Security
- All data is hosted on secure, enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure
- We use industry-standard encryption for data at rest
- Regular security audits and penetration testing

### Network Security
- All data transmission uses TLS 1.3 encryption (HTTPS)
- API endpoints are protected against common attacks
- DDoS protection and rate limiting

## Authentication Security

### Secure Sign-In
- Passwordless authentication via email OTP
- No passwords to steal or leak
- Session tokens with automatic expiration
- Secure token storage on your device

### Account Protection
- Automatic session invalidation on suspicious activity
- One active session per device
- Secure account deletion process

## Data Protection

### Encryption
- All personal data encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Database-level encryption

### Access Control
- Strict employee access controls
- Principle of least privilege
- Access logging and monitoring

### Location Privacy
- Location data processed in real-time only
- No persistent location storage
- Location used solely for venue discovery

## What We Don't Do

- We NEVER store your location history
- We NEVER sell your data to third parties
- We NEVER share data with advertisers
- We NEVER use dark patterns to collect data

## Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a security incident:
- We will notify affected users within 72 hours
- We will provide details on what data was affected
- We will take immediate remediation steps
- We will report to relevant authorities as required

## Your Security Practices

We recommend:
- Keep your device operating system updated
- Use device passcode or biometric lock
- Review app permissions periodically
- Log out if you lose your device (via account settings)

## Security Contact

To report security vulnerabilities:
- Email: hi@jointhirdspace.app
- Subject: "Security Report"
- We appreciate responsible disclosure

## Compliance

We follow industry best practices and comply with:
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- Apple App Store Guidelines
- Google Play Policies
