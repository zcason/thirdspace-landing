Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2025

Welcome to thirdspace. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, authentication, and important service communications
  • Display Name: Optional name you provide for personalization
  • User ID: Unique identifier generated for your account

1.2 Location Data

  • Precise Location: We collect your device's GPS coordinates to find nearby venues and provide location-based recommendations
  • Usage: Location data is used in real-time to match you with venues and verify check-ins. We do not store your location history
  • Control: You can disable location services at any time through your device settings, though this will limit core app functionality

1.3 Usage Data

  • Vibe preferences and mood selections
  • Venue interactions (views, matches, check-ins)
  • App usage patterns and feature engagement
  • Device information (model, OS version, app version)
  • Crash reports and error logs

1.4 Purchase Information

  • In-app purchase transactions through Apple App Store
  • Subscription status and expiration dates
  • We do not store your payment card information - this is handled securely by Apple

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • Find and recommend nearby venues based on your location and preferences
  • Process AI-powered vibe matching using your mood inputs
  • Track rewards, achievements, and check-ins
  • Manage subscription status and match quotas

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our recommendation algorithm
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user behavior
  • Optimize app performance and user experience

2.3 Communications

  • Send verification codes for authentication
  • Notify you about your matches and check-in opportunities (if notifications enabled)
  • Provide customer support responses
  • Send important service updates (with your consent)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our app:

3.1 Supabase (Database & Authentication)

  • Hosts user account data and app content
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

3.2 OpenAI (AI Vibe Matching)

  • Processes your mood text to generate vibe tags
  • We only send the text you provide, no identifying information
  • Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy

3.3 Mapbox (Maps & Location)

  • Provides map visualization and geocoding services
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.mapbox.com/privacy

3.4 Apple App Store (Payments)

  • Processes all in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy

4. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Location Data: Not stored; used in real-time only
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 2 years for analytics and service improvement
  • Transaction Data: Retained as required by law (typically 7 years)

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access & Portability

You have the right to request a copy of all personal data we hold about you in a machine-readable format.

5.2 Correction

You can update your email, display name, and preferences directly in the app settings.

5.3 Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data. Contact us at privacy@thirdspace.app to initiate deletion.

5.4 Opt-Out

  • Location: Disable in device settings
  • Push Notifications: Disable in app settings or device settings
  • Marketing Communications: Opt out through app settings

5.5 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR including:

  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Authentication tokens stored in device secure storage (iOS Keychain)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication for all systems

7. Children's Privacy

thirdspace is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to deletion
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:

Data Protection Officer: dpo@thirdspace.app

Response time: We aim to respond within 30 days
